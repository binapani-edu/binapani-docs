## 1. Brand Personality

### Core Principles

- **Meaningful Design**: Our design reflects our values and highlights key aspects of our brand.
- **Human-Centered Design**: Focus on user needs and feedback to create designs that resonate with our audience.
- **Accessibility**: Adhere to WCAG principles to ensure our content is accessible to all.

### Tone of Voice

- **Supportive**: Provide clear, practical insights and guidance.
- **Approachable**: Maintain a clean and focused design approach.
- **Confident**: Ensure clarity in our objectives and values.

## 3. Color Palette

Color is a vital component of Binapani's brand identity, symbolizing our innovative approach to education and our commitment to excellence. Our palette is designed to be versatile and impactful across all platforms and materials.

### Primary Colors

- **Binapani Dark Blue**
  - **Hex:** `#09476e`
  - **Usage:** Core brand color, used in logos, headers, and key brand elements to convey trust and professionalism.

- **Binapani Light Blue**
  - **Hex:** `#21bdfe`
  - **Usage:** Highlight color for calls-to-action, interactive elements, and important highlights to draw attention and engage users.

> **Note**: Use these colors consistently across all brand materials to maintain a cohesive brand identity.

### Secondary Colors

- **Binapani Accent Blue - Dark 1**
  - **Hex:** `#08466b`
  - **Usage:** Used for navigation backgrounds and structural elements to provide depth and contrast.

- **Binapani Accent Blue - Dark 2**
  - **Hex:** `#063451`
  - **Usage:** Used for navigation backgrounds and structural elements to provide depth and contrast.

- **Binapani Accent Blue - Light 1**
  - **Hex:** `#20bdff`
  - **Usage:** Backgrounds and subtle highlights to create a cohesive and modern aesthetic.

### Supporting Colors

- **White**
  - **Hex:** `#FFFFFF`
  - **Usage:** Primary background color, ensuring clarity and readability.

- **Text Colors**
  - **Primary Text:** `#000000` (Black)
  - **Secondary Text:** `#6F7CB2` (Muted Blue)

### Guidelines for Use

- **Consistency:** Use Binapani Blue and Accent Blue consistently across all brand materials to maintain a cohesive identity.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure sufficient contrast for text readability and distinguishable interactive elements.
- **Incorrect Use:** Avoid creating new color variations or altering the opacity of primary colors. Ensure color usage does not impair readability.

This color palette is designed to be applied across all Binapani products, websites, apps, and merchandise, ensuring a unified and recognizable brand presence.

### Incorrect Use

- Do not alter the colors or create new color variations.
- Ensure color usage does not impair readability.

## 5. Social Media Posts

- For all social media posts, Binapani logo and name should be used as a unified design element.
- For all social media posts, "Follow us on" followed by the social icons should be used as a unified design element.
- We encourage using the official assets provided in the design repository.
- Maintain at least 20px of spacing around the logo and text to ensure visual balance.
- All text used in social media posts must utilize the Roboto font family. If using a different font, please maintain uniformity in the font family for that post.
- Maintain appropriate font sizes to ensure readability across devices.
- The text and graphics content should be minimal and concise. It should follow the motto of "simplicity is the new sophistication".
- If the text content is more, try encorporating the main line in the text within the image of the post, and use the full text content in the text description of the post.
- Please use alt text for all images and graphics.
- Please use the correct hashtags and tags for the post.

## 6. Additional Guidelines

- **Aspect Ratios**: Design assets should cater to platform-specific dimensions.
- **Image Quality**: Use high-resolution images to avoid pixelation or blurriness.
- **Backgrounds**: Ensure the logo is displayed on a solid or minimal background for maximum contrast and visibility.
- **Hashtags and Tags**: Use approved hashtags and tag official Binapani accounts to maintain consistency in branding.
- **No Alterations**: Do not modify the logo or use unauthorized versions. This includes resizing disproportionately, adding effects, or changing colors.
